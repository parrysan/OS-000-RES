# What ACTUALLY Makes People Buy Things (Pricing Psychology Explained)

Price is not just a margin decision — it is a brand signal, a quality proxy, and a narrative device that shapes customer expectation before they ever try the product. Brands that underprice damage perception before they get a chance to exceed it. The same logic extends to product mix: every tier (entry, core, aspirational, halo) exists to make the others feel rational, not to maximise direct revenue from that SKU.
