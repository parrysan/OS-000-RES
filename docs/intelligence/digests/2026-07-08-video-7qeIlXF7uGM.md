# Copy This CRO Tutorial, It'll Blow Up Your Shopify Store

CRO is not button colours or competitor copying — it is customer understanding, with qualitative data over-indexed relative to quantitative. The single most actionable framework is identifying the 'metric on fire' (the one underperforming funnel stage) and directing all testing effort there, with every change traced to a specific data point from interviews, user testing, surveys, or review mining.
